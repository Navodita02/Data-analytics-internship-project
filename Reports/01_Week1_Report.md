# Week 1 Report – Outreach & Feedback Collection

## Objective

The objective of Week 1 was to introduce users to the Helllo App, encourage downloads and registrations, collect user feedback, and understand initial user impressions.

---

## Task Assigned

* Conduct outreach to users
* Explain Helllo App and its features
* Encourage app download and registration
* Collect feedback after usage
* Record responses in the provided sheet

---

## Work Completed

During Week 1, our team conducted user outreach and collected app downloads and feedback responses.

As Team Leader, I personally completed **50 user responses/downloads**, coordinated team activities, monitored progress, assigned work, and ensured timely completion of tasks.

Other team members also contributed by collecting responses, supporting outreach activities, and maintaining documentation according to assigned responsibilities.

The collected responses were later used for analysis and verification.

## Observations

* Users liked the idea of multiple services in one application
* Some users appreciated navigation and interface design
* Some users suggested increasing service availability
* Initial responses helped identify areas for deeper analysis

---

## Outcome

Week 1 helped build initial user understanding of the application and created the foundation for further analysis and response verification in later weeks.
